# **MALISHA – Privacy Policy**

**Last Updated:** [1/21/2026]

By **joining, remaining in, or interacting with a Discord server that has the MALISHA bot enabled**, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you should leave the server or refrain from interacting where MALISHA is active.

---

## **Consent & Scope**

MALISHA operates **at the server level**. Consent is considered granted when:

* You join a server where MALISHA is present (including, but not limited to, BLM servers)
* You continue to remain in the server after MALISHA is enabled
* You interact in channels where MALISHA has access

Server owners and administrators are responsible for ensuring members are informed that MALISHA is active.

---

## **1. Information We Collect**

MALISHA collects **only the minimum data required** to function as an AI-powered moderation and automation bot.

### **Data That May Be Processed**

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server (Guild) IDs
* Discord Channel IDs
* Message content (only when necessary for moderation, AI analysis, summaries, or logging)
* Moderation records (warnings, mutes, jails, bans, timestamps)
* Server-specific configuration settings

MALISHA does **not** monitor private DMs unless explicitly initiated by a command or feature enabled by staff.

---

## **2. Purpose of Data Processing**

Data is processed solely to:

* Enforce server rules
* Assist human moderation through AI analysis
* Log moderation actions
* Prevent repeat abuse or evasion
* Maintain security and reliability of the Bot

---

## **3. AI Processing Disclaimer**

MALISHA uses AI-based systems to assist moderation.
AI outputs may be incorrect, incomplete, or biased.

All moderation actions remain the responsibility of server staff.

---

## **4. Data Storage & Retention**

* Data is stored securely with restricted access
* Retention duration depends on server settings and enabled features
* Server data may be deleted after MALISHA is removed, unless required for abuse prevention or security

---

## **5. Data Sharing**

MALISHA does **not** sell or monetize user data.

Data may only be shared when:

* Required by law
* Necessary to protect against abuse or system harm
* Required to operate the Bot through trusted infrastructure providers

---

## **6. User Rights**

Where applicable, users may request:

* Access to their stored data
* Correction or deletion of data (subject to technical limitations)

Requests should be made through the official MALISHA support channel.

---

## **7. Children’s Privacy**

MALISHA is not intended for users under **13 years of age**, in accordance with Discord’s Terms of Service.

---

## **8. Policy Changes**

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued participation in servers using MALISHA constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

---
